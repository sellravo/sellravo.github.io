Shipping & returns at Sellravo

A quick overview of how we ship orders and handle returns.

Shipping

Handling time
Most orders ship within 1 business day.
Carriers
USPS and UPS with tracking on every package.
Shipping area
Currently shipping within the United States.
Packaging
Protective packing designed for collectibles and fragile items.

Returns & refunds

We aim to keep returns simple and fair.

  • Most items can be returned within 30 days of delivery.
  • Items should be returned in the same condition as received.
  • If an item arrives damaged or not as described, we work with you to make it right.

Damaged or incorrect items

If there is a problem with your order, please contact us first so we can review and help.

  • Contact us with your order number and a brief description.
  • Photos of the item and packaging help us resolve the issue faster.
  • When appropriate, we may provide return instructions or a replacement.

How to start a return

Returns are usually started through the same marketplace where you placed the order.

  • Open your order on eBay and select the return or message option.
  • Choose the reason and add a short note if needed.
  • We reply with next steps and keep you updated until the case is closed.
