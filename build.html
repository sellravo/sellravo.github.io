Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. This policy explains, in plain language, what information we handle and how we use it.

What this covers

  • Visits to this website (sellravo.store).
  • Messages you send directly to Sellravo by email or through marketplace platforms.
  • Order information we receive from marketplaces such as eBay.

Information we may receive

  • Contact details you choose to share (name, email address, mailing address, phone number).
  • Order details from the marketplace (items purchased, prices, shipment information).
  • Basic technical information from our hosting provider (such as IP address and browser type) stored in standard server logs.

How we use your information

  • To prepare, ship, and support your orders.
  • To respond to questions, requests, and return inquiries.
  • For internal record-keeping, accounting, and fraud-prevention checks.
  • To improve how we present and describe products.

Sharing of information

  • We share information only with service providers needed to complete your order, such as shipping carriers and marketplace payment processors.
  • We do not sell or rent your personal contact information to third parties for marketing.
  • Your payment details are processed by the marketplace or payment provider; Sellravo does not store full credit card numbers on this site.

Cookies and tracking

  • This site uses only basic, functional cookies and hosting-level logs needed to operate the website securely.
  • We do not run personalized advertising or third-party marketing trackers on this site.

Data retention

  • We keep order records and related messages for as long as reasonably needed for support, accounting, and legal record-keeping.
  • If you contact us about your information, we will review and, where appropriate, update or delete it, subject to any legal or tax requirements.

Your choices

  • You can email us at info@sellravo.store with privacy questions.
  • For information held by a marketplace (such as eBay), you can also manage your privacy settings directly through that platform.
